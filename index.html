Rameshwaram

Rameshwaram, one of the holiest places in India, is located on Pamban Island in Tamil Nadu. It is famous for its magnificent temples and pristine beaches.

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the most revered Char Dham pilgrimage sites, known for its magnificent corridors and 22 holy water tanks.

Dhanushkodi Beach

A pristine beach where the Bay of Bengal meets the Indian Ocean, offering stunning views and rich history.

Madurai

Madurai, often referred to as the 'Athens of the East', is known for its rich cultural heritage and historic temples.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

A historic Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi, known for its stunning architecture and colorful sculptures.

Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace

A 17th-century palace showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture with massive pillars and ornate arches.
